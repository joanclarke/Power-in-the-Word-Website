Welcome

Hello, welcome to Power In The Word Ministry. We are a church that is committed to building a people who understand that Jesus Christ must reign supreme in their lives. They must penetrate every area of national life, influencing it for righteousness. They must disciple the nations with the teachings of our Lord Jesus Christ and with practical engagements and involvement in the affairs of the nation, seeking the best welfare of the people. We hope you will join us!
