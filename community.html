Giving

/
Here at Power In The Word Ministry we give because God first gave to us. It’s not out of duty, obligation or guilt. Our gracious God actually rejects those motives. It’s not because we have to, it’s because we get to. And in giving out of a heart of gratitude we are able, in yet one more tangible way, to be part of a mission bigger than ourselves – sharing the Hope of Jesus with a world in need of Hope

Community Group

At Power in the Word Minitries, we believe that the church is God’s people existing in community together and not just a Sunday event! Because of this, we meet all throughout the week, all over the city in groups called Community Groups. This is a place where we do life together by caring for each other's needs, growing in our relationship with God, and sharing the Gospel of Jesus with the people God has placed in our lives. We share God's word by working through questions that are based on the Bible passage and the message given by the pastor that Sunday. Most of our groups eat together which includes full meals, light refreshments or deserts, based on the group.

Volunteer

Our Volunteer Groups meet in different places in more than 30 locations, all throughout each city. In our groups, our voluntarism vary from assisting with the elderly at homes to voluntaring our services at shelters and other not-for-profit organizations.

If you're not a part of a Volunteer or Community Group yet, but want to get connected, we would love to help you find a group that best fits your needs!
